Name: すき焼き
English Name: Sukiyaki
Country: Japan
Ingredients:
- Thinly sliced beef
- Shirataki noodles
- Shiitake mushrooms
- Grilled tofu
- Negi (Japanese leek)
- Shungiku (chrysanthemum greens)
- Warishita (soy-mirin-sake broth)
- Raw egg (for dipping)
Notes: Sukiyaki is the elegant cousin of hot pot — rich, sweet, and deeply comforting. Thin slices of premium beef are slowly simmered in a warishita broth made of soy sauce, sugar, and mirin alongside vegetables and tofu. The tradition of dipping the cooked beef into a bowl of raw egg may raise eyebrows abroad, but it creates an impossibly silky, custard-like coating that elevates every bite.
Recommended Restaurants:
- Imahan | Asakusa, Tokyo
- Matsuzaka | Ginza, Tokyo
