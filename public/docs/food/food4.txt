Name: 海南鸡饭
English Name: Hainanese Chicken Rice
Country: Singapore
Ingredients:
- Poached chicken
- Chicken-fat rice
- Chili sauce (sambal)
- Ginger paste
- Dark soy sauce
- Cucumber
- Pandan leaves
Notes: Singapore's unofficial national dish is a masterclass in simplicity. Silky poached chicken is served over rice cooked in rich chicken fat and pandan-infused broth, accompanied by three essential condiments: fiery chili sauce, pungent ginger paste, and sweet dark soy sauce. What looks plain on the surface hides layers of painstaking preparation — the result is one of the most satisfying meals on earth.
Recommended Restaurants:
- Tian Tian Hainanese Chicken Rice | Maxwell Food Centre, Singapore
- Boon Tong Kiat | Balestier, Singapore
